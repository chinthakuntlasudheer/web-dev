school

A school is the educational institution (and, in the case of in-person learning, the building) designed to provide learning environments for the teaching

education

the structured process of acquiring knowledge and skills, fostering intellectual and personal growth, and shaping moral values in individuals and society

teaching

Teaching is the profession and process of facilitating learning by imparting knowledge, skills, and values to students, fostering their growth and potential. It involves designing and guiding educational experiences, creating supportive environments, and using effective strategies to help learners acquire information and develop into well-rounded individuals. Key aspects include subject matter expertise, strong communication

sports

Sports are activities that involve physical skill and exertion, often in the form of a game, contest, or athletic event, governed by a set of rules and engaged in for entertainment, competition, or health benefits. They can be played by individuals or teams, indoors or outdoors, and serve to improve physical and mental fitness, develop personal qualities like teamwork

events

School events are organized activities that engage students, teachers, and families inacademic , cultural, or extracurricular pursuits, fostering a sense of community, enhancing social skills, and enriching the overall educational experience. These can include anything from daily classes and practicals to large-scale events like sports days, annual days, cultural festivals
