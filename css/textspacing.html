elections (Spacing)

An election is the formal process by which a population chooses representatives for public office or votes on a political proposition. A cornerstone of representative democracies, elections provide citizens a way to influence government policies, hold leaders accountable, and participate in political life.

caste (height)

A caste is ahereditary social group whose members are fixed by birth and often have an assigned occupation and social status

While the caste system originated in India's Hindu society, with its roots in ancient Vedas and concepts of varna, it has also influenced other religions and regions.
